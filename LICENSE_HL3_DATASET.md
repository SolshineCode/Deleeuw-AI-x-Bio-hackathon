## Access terms

Access to tier-3 data requires all of the following:

1. **Attestation.** You must attest that you are a biosecurity researcher, AI-safety researcher, or equivalent defensive practitioner, and that your intended use is defensive. The attestation elements are enumerated in [SAFETY.md §Tier-3 access attestation](SAFETY.md#tier-3-access-attestation).
2. **HL3.0 compliance.** All HL3.0 obligations apply — specifically, you must not use the data in ways that violate the UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights, the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, the ILO core labor standards, or the UN Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples.
3. **No public reproduction of specific prompts.** Tier-3 prompts are released as a dataset for audit and research use. They may not be reproduced verbatim in papers, blog posts, public repositories, talks, model outputs, or any other public artifact without prior written permission from the author. Aggregate statistics, redacted excerpts, and category-level summaries are fine and encouraged.
4. **Downstream obligation.** If you share the data onward — with a collaborator, a student, an institution — you must propagate the same attestation and no-public-reproduction requirements to the downstream recipient. Onward sharing does not terminate the gate.
